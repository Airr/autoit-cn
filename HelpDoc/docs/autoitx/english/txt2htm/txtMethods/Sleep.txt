###Method###
Sleep

###Description###
Pause script execution.

###Syntax###
Sleep delay


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
delay
	Amount of time to pause (in milliseconds).
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
None.


###Remarks###
Maximum sleep time is 2147483647 milliseconds (24 days).


###Related###
None.


###Example###
Set oAutoIt = WScript.CreateObject("AutoItX3.Control")
oAutoIt.Sleep 5000  'five seconds
