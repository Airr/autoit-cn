###Method###
WinMinimizeAllUndo

###Description###
Undoes a previous WinMinimizeAll function.

###Syntax###
WinMinimizeAllUndo


###Parameters###
None.

###ReturnValue###
None.


###Remarks###
Send("#+m") is a possible alternative.


###Related###
WinMinimizeAll, WinSetState


###Example###
Set oAutoIt = WScript.CreateObject("AutoItX3.Control")
oAutoIt.WinMinimizeAllUndo
