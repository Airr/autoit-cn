###Keyword###
#comments-start

###Description###
Specify that an entire section of script should be commented out.

###Syntax###
<strong>#comments-start</strong>
...
...
<strong>#comments-end</strong>


###Parameters###
None.

###Remarks###
The #comments-start and #comments-end directives can be nested.
You can also use the abbreviated keywords <strong>#cs</strong> and <strong>#ce</strong>.
Additionally, the directives themselves can be commented out!
