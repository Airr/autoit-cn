###Keyword###
With...EndWith

###Description###
Used to reduce long references to dot-accessible type of variables.

###Syntax###
<strong>With</strong> &lt;expression&gt;
	<em>statements</em>
	...
<strong>EndWith</strong>


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
expression
	Must be an object type expression
@@End@@

###Remarks###
With...EndWith statements may be nested.
