###Keyword###
With...EndWith

###Description###
Used to reduce long references to dot-accessible type of variables

###Syntax###
<b>With</b> &lt;expression&gt;
	<i>statements</i>
	...
<b>EndWith</b>


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
expression
	Must be an object type expression
@@End@@

###Remarks###
With...EndWith statements may be nested.
