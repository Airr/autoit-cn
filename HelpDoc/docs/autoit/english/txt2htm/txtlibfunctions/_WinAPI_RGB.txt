###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_RGB

###Description###
Creates a RGB color value based on red, green, and blue components.

###Syntax###
#include <WinAPIGdi.au3>
_WinAPI_RGB ( $iRed, $iGreen, $iBlue )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$iRed
	The intensity of the red color.
$iGreen
	The intensity of the green color.
$iBlue
	The intensity of the blue color.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns the resultant RGB color.
