###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlRebar_GetBandBackColor

###Description###
Retrieves the Band background color

###Syntax###
#include <GuiReBar.au3>
_GUICtrlRebar_GetBandBackColor ( $hWnd, $iIndex )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the rebar control
$iIndex
	0-based index of the band
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns the band background color.
