###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_PathAddLine

###Description###
Adds a line to the current figure of a path

###Syntax###
#include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_PathAddLine ( $hPath, $fX1, $fY1, $fX2, $fY2 )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hPath
	Pointer to a GraphicsPath object
$fX1
	The X coordinate of the starting point of the line
$fY1
	The Y coordinate of the starting point of the line
$fX2
	The X coordinate of the ending point of the line
$fY2
	The Y coordinate of the ending point of the line
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	True.
Failure:	False and sets the @error flag to non-zero, @extended may contain GPSTATUS ($GPID_ERR*).
@@End@@
