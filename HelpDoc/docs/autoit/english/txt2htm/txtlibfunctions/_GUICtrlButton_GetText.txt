_GUICtrlButton_GetText

###Syntax###
#include <GuiButton.au3>
_GUICtrlButton_GetText ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
