###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_PenGetColor

###Syntax###

_GDIPlus_PenGetColor ( $hPen )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hPen

@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
