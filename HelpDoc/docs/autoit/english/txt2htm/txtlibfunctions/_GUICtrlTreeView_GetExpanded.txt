###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetExpanded

###Description###
Indicates whether the item is expanded

###Syntax###
#include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetExpanded ( $hWnd, $hItem )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
True:	item is expanded.
False:	item is not expanded.
@@End@@

None.
