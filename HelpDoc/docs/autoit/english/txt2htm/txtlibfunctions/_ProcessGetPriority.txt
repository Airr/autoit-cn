###User Defined Function###
_ProcessGetPriority

###Description###
Get the  priority of an open process

###Syntax###
#include <Process.au3>
_ProcessGetPriority ( $PID )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$PID
	Process ID (Int) of process to be examined.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	an integer corressponding to the processes's priority:
		0 - Idle/Low
		1 - Below Normal
		2 - Normal
		3 - Above Normal
		4 - High
		5 - Realtime
Failure:	-1 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@
