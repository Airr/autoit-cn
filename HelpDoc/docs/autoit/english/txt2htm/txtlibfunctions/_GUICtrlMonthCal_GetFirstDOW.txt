###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetFirstDOW

###Description###
Retrieves the first day of the week

###Syntax###
#include <GuiMonthCal.au3>
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetFirstDOW ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: One of the following values:

	0 - Monday
	1 - Tuesday
	2 - Wednesday
	3 - Thursday
	4 - Friday
	5 - Saturday
	6 - Sunday


###Remarks###
None.


###Related###
_GUICtrlMonthCal_SetFirstDOW, _GUICtrlMonthCal_GetFirstDOWStr
