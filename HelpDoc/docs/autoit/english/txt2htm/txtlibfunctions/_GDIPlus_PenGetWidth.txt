###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_PenGetWidth

###Description###
Retrieve the width of a pen

###Syntax###
#include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_PenGetWidth ( $hPen )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hPen
	Handle to a pen object
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	the width of pen.
Failure:	-1 and sets the @error flag to non-zero, @extended may contain GPSTATUS ($GPID_ERR*).
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.
