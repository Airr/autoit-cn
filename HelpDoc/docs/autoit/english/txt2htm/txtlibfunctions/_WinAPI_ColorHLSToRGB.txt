###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_ColorHLSToRGB

###Description###
Converts colors from hue-luminance-saturation (HLS) to RGB format.

###Syntax###
#include <WinAPIGdi.au3>
_WinAPI_ColorHLSToRGB ( $iHue, $iLuminance, $iSaturation )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$iHue
	HLS hue value.
$iLuminance
	HLS luminance value.
$iSaturation
	HLS saturation value.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns the RGB value.
