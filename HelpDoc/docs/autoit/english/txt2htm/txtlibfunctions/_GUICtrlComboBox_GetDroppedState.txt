###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlComboBox_GetDroppedState

###Description###
Determines whether the ListBox of a ComboBox is dropped down

###Syntax###
#include <GuiComboBox.au3>
_GUICtrlComboBox_GetDroppedState ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
True:	Is dropped down.
False:	Not dropped down.
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.

_GUICtrlComboBox_ShowDropDown, _GUICtrlComboBoxEx_GetDroppedWidth
