###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_HiByte

###Description###
Returns the high BYTE of a 16-bit (2 bytes) value.

###Syntax###
#include <WinAPIMisc.au3>
_WinAPI_HiByte ( $iValue )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$iValue
	16-bit value.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
High BYTE value.


###Remarks###
None
