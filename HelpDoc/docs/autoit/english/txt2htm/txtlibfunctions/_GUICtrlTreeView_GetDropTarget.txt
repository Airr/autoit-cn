###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetDropTarget

###Description###
Indicates whether the item is drawn as a drag and drop target

###Syntax###
#include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetDropTarget ( $hWnd, $hItem )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
True:	item is drawn as a drop target.
False:	item is not drawn as a drop target.
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.
