###User Defined Function###

_GUICtrlListView_CopyItems ( $hWnd_Source, $hWnd_Destination [, $fDelFlag = False] )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd_Source

$hWnd_Destination
	Control ID/Handle to the control of the destination
$fDelFlag
