###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_GraphicsResetClip

###Description###

###Syntax###
#include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_GraphicsResetClip ( $hGraphics )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@

@@End@@
