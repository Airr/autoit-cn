###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetBold

###Description###
Indicates if the item is drawn in a bold style

###Syntax###
#include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetBold ( $hWnd, $hItem )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
True:	Item is drawn in bold
False:	Item is not drawn in bold
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.
