###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_Level

###Description###
Indicates the level of indentation of a item

###Syntax###
#include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_Level ( $hWnd, $hItem )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns the 0-based item indentation level.


###Remarks###
None.
