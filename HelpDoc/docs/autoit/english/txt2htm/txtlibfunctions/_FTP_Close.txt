###User Defined Function###
_FTP_Close

###Description###
Closes the _FTP_Open or _FTP_Connect session.

###Syntax###
#include <FTPEx.au3>
_FTP_Close ( $hSession )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hSession
	as returned by _FTP_Open() or _FTP_Connect()
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	1
Failure:	0
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.
