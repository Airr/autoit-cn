###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_GetStretchBltMode

###Description###

###Syntax###
#include <WinAPIGdi.au3>
_WinAPI_GetStretchBltMode ( $hDC )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hDC
	Handle to the device context.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
