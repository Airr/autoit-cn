###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_PenSetColor

###Description###
Sets the pen color

###Syntax###
#include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_PenSetColor ( $hPen, $iARGB )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hPen
	Handle to a pen object
$iARGB
	Alpha, Red, Green and Blue components of pen color
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	True.
Failure:	False and sets the @error flag to non-zero, @extended may contain GPSTATUS ($GPID_ERR*).
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.
