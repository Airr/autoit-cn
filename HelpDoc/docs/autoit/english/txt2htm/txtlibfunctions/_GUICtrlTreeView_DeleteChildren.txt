###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_DeleteChildren

###Description###
Deletes all children of a item

###Syntax###
#include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_DeleteChildren ( $hWnd, $hItem )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item whose children will be deleted
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	True
Failure:	False
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.
