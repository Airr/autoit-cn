###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_SwapQWord

###Description###
Converts a ULONGLONG from little-endian to big-endian, and vice versa

###Syntax###
#include <WinAPIMisc.au3>
_WinAPI_SwapQWord ( $iValue )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$iValue
	The ULONGLONG value to convert.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns the converted value.
