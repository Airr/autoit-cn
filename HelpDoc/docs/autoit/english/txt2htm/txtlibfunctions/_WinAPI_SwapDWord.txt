###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_SwapDWord

###Description###
Converts a ULONG from little-endian to big-endian, and vice versa.

###Syntax###
#include <WinAPIMisc.au3>
_WinAPI_SwapDWord ( $iValue )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$iValue
	The ULONG value to convert.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns the converted value.
