###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlToolbar_GetInsertMarkColor

###Description###

###Syntax###
#include <GuiToolbar.au3>
_GUICtrlToolbar_GetInsertMarkColor ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
