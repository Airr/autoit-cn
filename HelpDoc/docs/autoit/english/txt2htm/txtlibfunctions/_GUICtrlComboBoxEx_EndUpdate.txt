###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_EndUpdate

###Description###

###Syntax###

_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_EndUpdate ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
