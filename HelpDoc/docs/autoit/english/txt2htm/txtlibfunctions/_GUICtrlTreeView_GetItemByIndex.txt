###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetItemByIndex

###Description###
Retrieve a item by its position in the list of child items

###Syntax###
#include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetItemByIndex ( $hWnd, $hItem, $iIndex )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item
$iIndex
	0-based index of item in the list of child items
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	the handle of the item.
Failure:	0.
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.
