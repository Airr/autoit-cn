###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_EqualRgn

###Description###
Checks the two specified regions to determine whether they are identical.

###Syntax###
#include <WinAPIGdi.au3>
_WinAPI_EqualRgn ( $hRgn1, $hRgn2 )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hRgn1
	Handle to a region.
$hRgn2
	Handle to a region.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success 	True - The two regions are equal.
Failure 	False.
@@End@@
