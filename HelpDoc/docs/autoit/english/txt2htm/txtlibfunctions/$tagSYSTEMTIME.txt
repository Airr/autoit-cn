###Structure Name###
$tagSYSTEMTIME

###Description###
Specifies a date and time, in coordinated universal time (UTC)

###Syntax###
Global Const $tagSYSTEMTIME = "struct; word Year;word Month;word Dow;word Day;word Hour;word Minute;word Second;word MSeconds; endstruct"


###Fields###
@@ParamTable@@
Year

Month
	Month
Dow
	Day of week
Day
	Day
Hour
	Hour
Minute
	Minute
Second
	Second
MSeconds
	Milliseconds
@@End@@
