###User Defined Function###
_FTP_FileClose

###Description###
Closes the Handle returned by _FTP_FileOpen.

###Syntax###
#include <FTPEx.au3>
_FTP_FileClose ( $hFTPFile )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hFTPFile
	as returned by _FTP_FileOpen()
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	1.
Failure:	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.

_FTP_FileOpen
