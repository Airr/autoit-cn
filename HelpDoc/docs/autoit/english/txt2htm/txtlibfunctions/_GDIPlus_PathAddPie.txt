###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_PathAddPie

###Description###
Adds a pie to a path

###Syntax###
#include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_PathAddPie ( $hPath, $fX, $fY, $fWidth, $fHeight, $fStartAngle, $fSweepAngle )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hPath
	Pointer to a GraphicsPath object
$fX
	The X coordinate of the upper left corner of the rectangle that bounds the ellipse that bounds
	the pie
$fY
	The Y coordinate of the upper left corner of the rectangle that bounds the ellipse that bounds
	the pie
$fWidth
	The width of the rectangle that bounds the ellipse that bounds the pie
$fHeight
	The height of the rectangle that bounds the ellipse that bounds the pie
$fStartAngle
	The angle, in degrees, between the X axis and the starting point of the arc that defines the
	pie. A positive value specifies clockwise rotation.
$fSweepAngle
	The angle, in degrees, between the starting and ending points of the arc that defines the pie.
	A positive value specifies clockwise rotation.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: True
Failure: False
