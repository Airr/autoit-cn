###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlMenu_SetMenuHeight

###Description###

###Syntax###
#include <GuiMenu.au3>
_GUICtrlMenu_SetMenuHeight ( $hMenu, $iHeight )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hMenu
	Handle of the menu
$iHeight
	Maximum height of the menu
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
