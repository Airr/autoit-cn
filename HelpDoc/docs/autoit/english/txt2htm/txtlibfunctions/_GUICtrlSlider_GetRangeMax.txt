###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlSlider_GetRangeMax

###Description###
Retrieves the maximum position for the slider

###Syntax###
#include <GuiSlider.au3>
_GUICtrlSlider_GetRangeMax ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns the maximum position in the slider's range of minimum to maximum slider positions.


###Remarks###
None.


###Related###
_GUICtrlSlider_GetRange, _GUICtrlSlider_SetRangeMax
