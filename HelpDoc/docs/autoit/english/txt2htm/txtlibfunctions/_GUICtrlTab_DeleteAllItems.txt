###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTab_DeleteAllItems

###Description###
Deletes all tabs

###Syntax###
#include <GuiTab.au3>
_GUICtrlTab_DeleteAllItems ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	True.
Failure:	False.
@@End@@


###Remarks###
This does not delete the controls on the tabitems.
