###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_PathIterRewind

###Description###
Rewinds an iterator to the beginning of its associated path

###Syntax###
#include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_PathIterRewind ( $hPathIter )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hPathIter
	Pointer to a GraphicsPathIterator object
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	True.
Failure:	False and sets the @error flag to non-zero, @extended may contain GPSTATUS ($GPID_ERR*).
@@End@@

None.
