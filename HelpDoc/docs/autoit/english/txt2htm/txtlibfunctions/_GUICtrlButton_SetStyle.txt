###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlButton_SetStyle

###Description###
Sets the style of a button

###Syntax###
#include <GuiButton.au3>
_GUICtrlButton_SetStyle ( $hWnd, $iStyle )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
$iStyle
	Can be a combination of button styles
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
None.
