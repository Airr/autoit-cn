###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_GetComboControl

###Syntax###
#include <GuiComboBoxEx.au3>
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_GetComboControl ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
