###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlEdit_GetMargins

###Description###
Retrieves the widths of the left and right margins

###Syntax###
#include <GuiEdit.au3>
_GUICtrlEdit_GetMargins ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Array in the following format:

	[0] - The width of the left margin
	[1] - The width of the right margin


###Remarks###
None.
