###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlHeader_GetItemAlign

###Description###
Retrieves the item text alignment

###Syntax###
#include <GuiHeader.au3>
_GUICtrlHeader_GetItemAlign ( $hWnd, $iIndex )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iIndex
	0-based item index
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	Item text alignment:
			0 - Left
			1 - Right
			2 - Center
Failure:	-1.
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.
