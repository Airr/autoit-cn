###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_Beep

###Description###
Generates simple tones on the speaker

###Syntax###
#include <WinAPI.au3>
_WinAPI_Beep ( [$iFreq = 500 [, $iDuration = 1000]] )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$iFreq
	[optional] The frequency of the sound, in hertz. This parameter must be in the range 37 through 32,767.
$iDuration
	[optional] The duration of the sound, in milliseconds. Windows Me/98/95: This parameter is ignored.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	True
Failure:	False
@@End@@


###Remarks###
Muting and volume control have no effect on Beep. You will still hear the tone.
