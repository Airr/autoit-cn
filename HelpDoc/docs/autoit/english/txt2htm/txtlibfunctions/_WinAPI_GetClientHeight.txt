###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_GetClientHeight

###Description###
Retrieves the height of a window's client area

###Syntax###
#include <WinAPI.au3>
_WinAPI_GetClientHeight ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle of window
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	the client area height.
Failure:	sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.
