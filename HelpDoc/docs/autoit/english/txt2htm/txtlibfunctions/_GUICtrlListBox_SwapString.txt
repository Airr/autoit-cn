###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlListBox_SwapString

###Description###
Swaps the text of two items at the specified indices

###Syntax###
#include <GuiListBox.au3>
_GUICtrlListBox_SwapString ( $hWnd, $iIndexA, $iIndexB )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
$iIndexA
	Zero-based index item to swap
$iIndexB
	Zero-based index item to swap
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	True
Failure:	False
@@End@@
