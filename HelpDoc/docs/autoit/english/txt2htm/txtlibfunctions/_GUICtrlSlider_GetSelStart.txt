###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlSlider_GetSelStart

###Description###
Retrieves the starting position of the current selection range

###Syntax###
#include <GuiSlider.au3>
_GUICtrlSlider_GetSelStart ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: The starting position of the current selection range
