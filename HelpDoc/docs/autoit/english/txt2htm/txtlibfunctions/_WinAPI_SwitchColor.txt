###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_SwitchColor

###Description###
Converts a color from BGR to RGB and vice versa.

###Syntax###
#include <WinAPIGdi.au3>
_WinAPI_SwitchColor ( $iColor )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$iColor
	The color to conversion.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns the converted color (RGB or BGR - depends on the $iColor value, BGR > RGB > BGR etc).
