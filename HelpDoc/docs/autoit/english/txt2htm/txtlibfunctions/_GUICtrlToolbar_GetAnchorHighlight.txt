###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlToolbar_GetAnchorHighlight

###Description###
Retrieves the anchor highlight setting

###Syntax###
#include <GuiToolbar.au3>
_GUICtrlToolbar_GetAnchorHighlight ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
True:	anchor highlighting is enabled.
False:	anchor highlighting is not enabled?
@@End@@


###Remarks###
Anchor highlighting means that the last highlighted item will remain highlighted until another item is highlighted.
This occurs even if the cursor leaves the toolbar control.
