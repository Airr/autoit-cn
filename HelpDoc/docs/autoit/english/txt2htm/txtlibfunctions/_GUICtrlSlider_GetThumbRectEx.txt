###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlSlider_GetThumbRectEx

###Description###
Retrieves the size and position of the bounding rectangle for the slider

###Syntax###
#include <GuiSlider.au3>
_GUICtrlSlider_GetThumbRectEx ( $hWnd )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Control ID/Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns a $tagRECT structure that receives the bounding rectangle of the slider in client coordinates.


###Remarks###
None.


###Related###
$tagRECT, _GUICtrlSlider_GetThumbRect
