###Function###
Sin

###Description###
Calculates the sine of a number.

###Syntax###
Sin ( expression )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
expression
	Value in radians.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns the trigonometric sine of the number.


###Remarks###
1° = pi / 180 radians.


###Related###
Cos, Tan, ASin, ACos, ATan
