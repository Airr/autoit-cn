###Function###
Exp

###Description###
Calculates e to the power of a number.

###Syntax###
Exp ( expression )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
expression
	Any valid numeric expression.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns <em><strong>e</strong></em> raised to the power of <em>expression</em>.


###Remarks###
<em><strong>e</strong></em> is the base of the natural logarithm and is approximately 2.71828182845905
