###Function###
TrayItemDelete

###Description###
Deletes a menu/item control from the tray menu.

###Syntax###
TrayItemDelete ( controlID )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
controlID
	The control identifier (controlID) as returned by a <a href="../functions/TrayCreateItem.htm">TrayCreateItem</a> or <a href="../functions/TrayCreateMenu.htm">TrayCreateMenu</a> function.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	1.
Failure:	0.
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.


###Related###
TrayCreateItem, TrayCreateMenu
