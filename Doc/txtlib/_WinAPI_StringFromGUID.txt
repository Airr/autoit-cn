###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_StringFromGUID

###Description###
Converts a binary GUID to string form

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPI.au3>
_WinAPI_StringFromGUID($pGUID)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$pGUID
	Pointer to a $tagGUID structure
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: GUID in string form

###Remarks###
None.
