###User Defined Function###
_Date_Time_SystemTimeToArray

###Description###
Decode a system time to an array

###Syntax###
#Include <Date.au3>
_Date_Time_SystemTimeToArray(ByRef $tSystemTime)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$tSystemTime
	$tagSYSTEMTIME structure
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Array with the following format:
		[0] - Month
		[1] - Day
		[2] - Year
		[3] - Hour
		[4] - Minutes
		[5] - Seconds
		[6] - Milliseconds
		[7] - Day of week

###Remarks###
None.
