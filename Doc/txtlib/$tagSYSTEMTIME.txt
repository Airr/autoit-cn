###Structure Name###
$tagSYSTEMTIME

###Description###
Specifies a date and time, in coordinated universal time (UTC)

###Syntax###
Global Const $tagSYSTEMTIME = "short Year;short Month;short Dow;short Day;short Hour;short Minute;short Second;short MSeconds"
