###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlToolbar_GetTextRows

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiToolBar.au3>
_GUICtrlToolbar_GetTextRows($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
