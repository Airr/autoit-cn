###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlMenu_SetMenuContextHelpID

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiMenu.au3>
_GUICtrlMenu_SetMenuContextHelpID($hMenu, $iHelpID)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hMenu
	Handle of the menu
$iHelpID
	Context help ID
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
