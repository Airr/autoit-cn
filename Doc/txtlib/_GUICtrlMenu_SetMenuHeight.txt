###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlMenu_SetMenuHeight

###Description###
Sets the maximum height of the menu

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiMenu.au3>
_GUICtrlMenu_SetMenuHeight($hMenu, $iHeight)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hMenu
	Handle of the menu
$iHeight
	Maximum height of the menu
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
成功: True
失败: False
