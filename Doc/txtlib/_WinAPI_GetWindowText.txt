###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_GetWindowText

###Description###
Retrieves the text of the specified window's title bar

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPI.au3>
_WinAPI_GetWindowText($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle of the window
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Windows title bar
