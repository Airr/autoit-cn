###User Defined Function###
_Security__GetLengthSid

###Description###
Returns the length, in bytes, of a valid SID

###Syntax###
#Include <Security.au3>
_Security__GetLengthSid($pSID)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$pSID
	Pointer to a SID
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Length of SID
Failure: 0
