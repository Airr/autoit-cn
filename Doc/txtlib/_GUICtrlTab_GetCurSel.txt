###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTab_GetCurSel

###Description###
Determines the currently selected tab

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTab.au3>
_GUICtrlTab_GetCurSel($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: The zero based index of the selected tab
Failure: -1

###Remarks###
None.
