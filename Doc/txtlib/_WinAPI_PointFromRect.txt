###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_PointFromRect

###Description###
Returns the top/left coordinates of a $tagRECT as a $tagPOINT structure

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPI.au3>
_WinAPI_PointFromRect(ByRef $tRect[, $fCenter = True])

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$tRect
	$tagRECT structure
$fCenter
	If True, the return will be a point at the center of the rectangle, otherwise the left/top
		coordinates are returned.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: $tagPOINT structure

###Remarks###
This function is used to get the click position for many of the click functions
