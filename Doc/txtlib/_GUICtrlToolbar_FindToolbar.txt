###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlToolbar_FindToolbar

###Description###
Finds a specific toolbar

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiToolBar.au3>
_GUICtrlToolbar_FindToolbar($hWnd, $sText)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Window handle or text of window
$sText
	Button text to search for
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Handle of the toolbar
Failure: 0
