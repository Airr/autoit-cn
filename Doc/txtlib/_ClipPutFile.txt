###User Defined Function###
_ClipPutFile

###Description###
Copy Files to Clipboard Like Explorer does

###Syntax###
#Include <Misc.au3>
_ClipPutFile($sFile[, $sSeperator = "|"])

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$sFile
	Full Path to File(s)
$sSeperator
	Seperator for multiple Files, Default = '|'
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: True
Failure: False and Sets @ERROR to:

	1 - Unable to Open Clipboard
	2 - Unable to Empty Cipboard
	3 - GlobalAlloc Failed
	4 - GlobalLock Failed
	5 - Unable to Create H_DROP
	6 - Unable to Update Clipboard
	7 - Unable to Close Clipboard
	8 - GlobalUnlock Failed

###Remarks###
None.
