###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_DeleteChildren

###Description###
Deletes all children of a item

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_DeleteChildren($hWnd, $hItem)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item whose children will be deleted
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: True
Failure: False

###Remarks###
None.
