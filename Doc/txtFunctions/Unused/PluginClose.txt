###Function###
PluginClose

###Description###
Close a plugin file

###Syntax###
PluginClose ( dllhandle )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
dllhandle
	The handle of a dll, as returned by a previous call to PluginOpen.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功:	返回值为 1.
失败:	返回值为 0.
@@End@@


###Remarks###
None.
