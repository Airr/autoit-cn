###User Defined Function###
_DateIsLeapYear

###Description###
Checks a given year to see if it is a leap year.

###Syntax###
#Include <Date.au3>
_DateIsLeapYear($iYear)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$iYear
	The 4-digit year to check.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	Returns 1.
Failure:	Returns 0 if the year is not a leap year and Sets @error
@error:	0 - No error.
	1 - Invalid year.
@@End@@
