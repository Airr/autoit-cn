###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_PathUnquoteSpaces

###Description###
Removes quotes from the beginning and end of a path.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_PathUnquoteSpaces ( $sPath )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$sPath
	The path.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	The path with beginning and ending quotation marks removed.
Failure	Empty string and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@
