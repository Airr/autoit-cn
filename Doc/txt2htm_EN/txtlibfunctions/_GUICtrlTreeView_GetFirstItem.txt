###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetFirstItem

###Description###
Retrieves the topmost or very first item

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetFirstItem($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	The handle of the first item
Failure:	0
@@End@@
