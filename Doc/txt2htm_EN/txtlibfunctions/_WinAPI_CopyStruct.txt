###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_CopyStruct

###Description###
Creates a duplicate of a specified structure.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_CopyStruct (ByRef $tStruct [, $sStruct] )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$tStruct
	The structure to be duplicated.
$sStruct
	[可选参数] The string representing the structure (same as for the DllStructCreate()).
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	"byte[n]" or $sStruct structure that was created.
Failure	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@
