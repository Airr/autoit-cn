###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_RectIsEmpty

###Description###
Determins whether a rectangle is empty

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPI.au3>
_WinAPI_RectIsEmpty(ByRef $tRect)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$tRect
	$tagRect structure
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
True:	Rectangle is empty (all values are zero)
False:	Rectangle is not empty
@@End@@
