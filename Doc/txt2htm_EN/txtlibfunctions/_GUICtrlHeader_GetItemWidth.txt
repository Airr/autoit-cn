###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlHeader_GetItemWidth

###Description###
Retrieves the item's width

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiHeader.au3>
_GUICtrlHeader_GetItemWidth($hWnd, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$iIndex
	Zero based item index
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Width of the item
