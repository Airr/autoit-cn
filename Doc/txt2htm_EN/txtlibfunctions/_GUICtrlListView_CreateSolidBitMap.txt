###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlListView_CreateSolidBitMap

###Description###
Creates a solid color bitmap

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiListView.au3>
_GUICtrlListView_CreateSolidBitMap($hWnd, $iColor, $iWidth, $iHeight)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the window where the bitmap will be displayed
$iColor
	The color of the bitmap, stated in RGB
$iWidth
	The width of the bitmap
$iHeight
	The height of the bitmap
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns an Handle to the bitmap

###Remarks###
None.
