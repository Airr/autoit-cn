###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTab_GetItemImage

###Description###
Retrieves the image index for a tab

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTab.au3>
_GUICtrlTab_GetItemImage($hWnd, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iIndex
	Zero based item index
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Zero based image index of the tab

###Remarks###
None.
