###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_CloseWindow

###Description###
Minimizes (but does not destroy) the specified window.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_CloseWindow ( $hWnd )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the window to be minimized.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	1.
Failure	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@

None
