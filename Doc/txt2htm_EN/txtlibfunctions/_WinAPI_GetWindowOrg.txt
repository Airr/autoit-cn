###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_GetWindowOrg

###Description###
Retrieves the x-coordinates and y-coordinates of the window origin for the specified device context.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_GetWindowOrg ( $hDC )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hDC
	Handle to the device context.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	$tagPOINT structure that receives the coordinates, in logical units, of the window origin.
Failure	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@

###Remarks###
None
