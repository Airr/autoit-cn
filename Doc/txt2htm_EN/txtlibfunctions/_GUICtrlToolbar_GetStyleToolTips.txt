###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlToolbar_GetStyleToolTips

###Description###
Indicates that the control has tooltips

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiToolBar.au3>
_GUICtrlToolbar_GetStyleToolTips($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
True: Style is present
False: Style is not present

###Remarks###
None.
