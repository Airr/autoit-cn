###Structure Name###
$tagPOINT

###Description###
Defines the x- and y- coordinates of a point

###Syntax###
Global Const $tagPOINT = "int X;int Y"

###Fields###
@@ParamTable@@
X
	Specifies the x-coordinate of the point
Y
	Specifies the y-coordinate of the point
@@End@@

###Remarks###
None.
