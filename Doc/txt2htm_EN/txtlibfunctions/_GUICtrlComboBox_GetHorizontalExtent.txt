###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlComboBox_GetHorizontalExtent

###Description###
Gets the width, in pixels, that the ListBox of a ComboBox control can be scrolled horizontally

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiComboBox.au3>
_GUICtrlComboBox_GetHorizontalExtent($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: The scrollable width, in pixels

###Remarks###
None.
