###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_GetClientHeight

###Description###
Retrieves the height of a window's client area

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPI.au3>
_WinAPI_GetClientHeight($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@

	Handle of window
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###

Failure:	set @error
@@End@@
