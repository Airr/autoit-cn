###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_EqualStruct

###Description###
Determines whether the two specified structures are equal.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_EqualStruct (ByRef $tStruct1, ByRef $tStruct2)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$tStruct1
	The structure that contains the first source data.
$tStruct2
	The structure that contains the second source data.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	1 - The two structures are identical.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;0	Otherwise.
Failure	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@
