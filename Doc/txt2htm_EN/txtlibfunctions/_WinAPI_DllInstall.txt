###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_DllInstall

###Description###
Registers OLE controls such as DLL or ActiveX Controls (OCX) files.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_DllInstall ( $sPath )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$sPath
	The path to the DLL file that will be registered.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	1.
Failure	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@
