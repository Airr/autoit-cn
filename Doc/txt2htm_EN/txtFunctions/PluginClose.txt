###Function###

###Experimental###

###Description###

###Syntax###
PluginClose ( dllhandle )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
dllhandle
	The handle of a dll, as returned by a previous call to PluginOpen.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	Returns 1.
Failure:	Returns 0.
@@End@@
