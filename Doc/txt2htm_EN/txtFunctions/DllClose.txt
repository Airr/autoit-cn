###Function###
DllClose

###Description###
Closes a previously opened DLL.

###Syntax###
DllClose ( dllhandle )


###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
dllhandle
	The handle of a dll, as returned by a previous call to DllOpen.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
None.


###Remarks###
Upon termination, AutoIt automatically closes any dlls it opened but calling DllClose is still a good idea.


###Related###
DllCall, DllOpen
