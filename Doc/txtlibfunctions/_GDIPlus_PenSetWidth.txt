###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_PenSetWidth

###Syntax###
#Include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_PenSetWidth($hPen, $nWidth)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@

$nWidth
	Width of pen
@@End@@
