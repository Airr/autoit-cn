###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlSlider_GetRangeMin

###Description###
Retrieves the minimum position for the slider

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiSlider.au3>
_GUICtrlSlider_GetRangeMin($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: The minimum position in the slider's range of minimum to maximum slider positions

###Remarks###
None.
