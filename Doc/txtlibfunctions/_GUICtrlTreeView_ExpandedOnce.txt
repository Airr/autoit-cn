###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_ExpandedOnce

###Description###
Indicates if the item's list of child items has been expanded at least once

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_ExpandedOnce($hWnd, $hItem)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	控件句柄
$hItem
	项目句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
True:	Item has been expanded at least once
False:	Item has not been expanded at least once
@@End@@

###Remarks###
None.
