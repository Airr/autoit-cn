###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlRebar_ShowBand

###Description###
Shows or hides a given band in a rebar control

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiRebar.au3>
_GUICtrlRebar_ShowBand($hWnd, $iIndex[, $fShow = True])

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to rebar control
$iIndex
	Zero-based index of the band
$fShow
	[optional] indicates if the band should be shown or hidden:
		 True - Show
		False - Hide
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
None.

###Remarks###
None.
