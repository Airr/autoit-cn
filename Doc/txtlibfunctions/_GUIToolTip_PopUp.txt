###User Defined Function###
_GUIToolTip_PopUp

###Description###
Causes the ToolTip to display at the coordinates of the last mouse message

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiToolTip.au3>
_GUIToolTip_PopUp($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
None.

###Remarks###
Only available on Windows XP
