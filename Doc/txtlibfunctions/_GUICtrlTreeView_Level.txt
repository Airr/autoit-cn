###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_Level

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_Level($hWnd, $hItem)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$hItem

@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Zero based item indentation level
