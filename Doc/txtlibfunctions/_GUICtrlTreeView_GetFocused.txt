###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetFocused

###Description###
Indicates whether the item appears to have focus

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetFocused($hWnd, $hItem)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
True: Item appears to have focus
False: Item does not appear to have focus

###Remarks###
None.
