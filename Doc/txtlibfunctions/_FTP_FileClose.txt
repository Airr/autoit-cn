###User Defined Function###
_FTP_FileClose

###Description###
Closes the Handle returned by _FTP_FileOpen.

###Syntax###
#Include <FTPEx.au3>
_FTP_FileClose($l_InternetSession)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$l_InternetSession
	as returned by _FTP_FileOpen()
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	1
Failure:	0 and sets @error to non-zero
@@End@@

###Remarks###
None.

_FTP_FileOpen
