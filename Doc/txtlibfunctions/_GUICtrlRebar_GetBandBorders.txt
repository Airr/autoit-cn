###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlRebar_GetBandBorders

###Description###
Retrieves the borders of a band. The result of this message can be used to calculate the usable area in a band

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiRebar.au3>
_GUICtrlRebar_GetBandBorders($hWnd, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to rebar control
$iIndex
	Zero-based index of the band for which the borders will be retrieved
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Returns an Array with the following format:
	[0] - X coordinate of the upper left corner of the rectangle
	[1] - Y coordinate of the upper left corner of the rectangle
	[2] - X coordinate of the lower right corner of the rectangle
	[3] - Y coordinate of the lower right corner of the rectangle

###Remarks###
None.
