###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_GUIDFromString

###Description###
从字符串GUID转换为二进制GUID.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPI.au3>
_WinAPI_GUIDFromString($sGUID)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$sGUID
	字符串GUID
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
成功: 返回 $tagGUID 数据结构

None.
