###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_SelectItemByIndex

###Description###

_GUICtrlTreeView_SelectItemByIndex($hWnd, $hItem, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@

	Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item
$iIndex

@@End@@
