###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlMonthCal_SetRange

###Description###
Sets date information that represents the high and low limits

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiMonthCal.au3>
_GUICtrlMonthCal_SetRange($hWnd, $iMinYear, $iMinMonth, $iMinDay, $iMaxYear, $iMaxMonth, $iMaxDay)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$iMinYear
	Minimum year
$iMinMonth
	Minimum month
$iMinDay
	Minimum day
$iMaxYear
	Maximum year
$iMaxMonth
	Maximum month
$iMaxDay
	Maximum day
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: True
Failure: False

###Remarks###
None.
