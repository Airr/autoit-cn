###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_DeleteString

###Description###
Removes an item from a ComboBoxEx control

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiComboBoxEx.au3>
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_DeleteString($hWnd, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iIndex
	Zero based index of the Item to delete
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Number of items remaining in the control

###Remarks###
None.
