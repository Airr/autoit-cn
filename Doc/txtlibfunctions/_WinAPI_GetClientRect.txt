###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_GetClientRect

###Description###
Retrieves the coordinates of a window's client area

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPI.au3>
_WinAPI_GetClientRect($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle of window
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	$tagRECT structure that receives the client coordinates
Failure:	set @error
@@End@@

###Remarks###
None.
