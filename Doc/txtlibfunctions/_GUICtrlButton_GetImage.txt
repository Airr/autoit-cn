###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlButton_GetImage

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiButton.au3>
_GUICtrlButton_GetImage($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	控件句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
成功: 返回图像句柄
失败: 0
