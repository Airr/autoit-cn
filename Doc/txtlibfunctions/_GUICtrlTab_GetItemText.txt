###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTab_GetItemText

###Description###
Retrieves the text of a tab

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTab.au3>
_GUICtrlTab_GetItemText($hWnd, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iIndex
	Zero based item index
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Tab text
