###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlRichEdit_Cut

###Description###
Cut text to clipboard

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiRichEdit.au3>
_GUICtrlRichEdit_Cut($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	True
Failure:	False and sets @error

@@End@@
