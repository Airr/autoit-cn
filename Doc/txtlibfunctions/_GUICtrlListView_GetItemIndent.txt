###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlListView_GetItemIndent

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiListView.au3>
_GUICtrlListView_GetItemIndent($hWnd, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@

$iIndex

@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
