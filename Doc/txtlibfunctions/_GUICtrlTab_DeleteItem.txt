###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTab_DeleteItem

###Description###
Deletes a tab

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTab.au3>
_GUICtrlTab_DeleteItem($hWnd, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$iIndex
	Zero based index of the item
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
成功: True
失败: False

###Remarks###
This does not delete the controls on the tabitems
