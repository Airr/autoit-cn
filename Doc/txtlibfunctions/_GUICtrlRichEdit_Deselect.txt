###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlRichEdit_Deselect

###Description###
Deselects text, leaving none selected

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiRichEdit.au3>
_GUICtrlRichEdit_Deselect($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	True
Failure:	False and sets @error
@error:	101 - $hWnd is not a handle
@@End@@

###Remarks###
Leaves the insertion point at the anchor point of the selection

_GUICtrlRichEdit_SetSel, _GUICtrlRichEdit_GetSel, _GUICtrlRichEdit_IsTextSelected
