###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_BeginUpdate

###Description###
Prevents updating of the control until the EndUpdate function is called

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_BeginUpdate($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@

失败:	False
@@End@@
