###User Defined Function###
_GUIImageList_GetImageInfoEx

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiImageList.au3>
_GUIImageList_GetImageInfoEx($hWnd, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd

$iIndex
	Index of the image
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@

@@End@@
