###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_Shutdown

###Description###
Clean up resources used by Microsoft Windows GDI+

###Syntax###
#Include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_Shutdown()

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
None.

@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: True
Failure: False

###Remarks###
You must dispose of all of your GDI+ objects before you call _GDIPlus_Shutdown
