###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_Shutdown

###Description###
Clean up resources used by Microsoft Windows GDI+

###Syntax###
#Include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_Shutdown()

###Parameters###
None.


###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功:	True
失败:	False
@@End@@

###Remarks###
You must dispose of all of your GDI+ objects before you call _GDIPlus_Shutdown
