###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_RectFCreate

###Description###
Create a $tagGDIPRECTF structure

###Syntax###
#Include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_RectFCreate([$nX = 0[, $nY = 0[, $nWidth = 0[, $nHeight = 0]]]])

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$nX
	X coordinate of upper left hand corner of rectangle
$nY
	Y coordinate of upper left hand corner of rectangle
$nWidth
	Rectangle width
$nHeight
	Rectangle height
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: $tagGDIPRECTF structure

###Remarks###
None.

$tagGDIPRECTF
