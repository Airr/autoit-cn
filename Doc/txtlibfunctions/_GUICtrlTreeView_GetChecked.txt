###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetChecked

###Description###
Indicates if a item has its checkbox checked

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetChecked($hWnd, $hItem)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
True: Item's checkbox is checked
False: Item's checkbox is not checked

###Remarks###
None.
