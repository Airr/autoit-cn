###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlRichEdit_Redo

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiRichEdit.au3>
_GUICtrlRichEdit_Redo($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@

Failure:	False and sets @error

@@End@@
