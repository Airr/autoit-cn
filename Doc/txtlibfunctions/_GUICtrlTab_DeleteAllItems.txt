###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTab_DeleteAllItems

###Description###
Deletes all tabs

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTab.au3>
_GUICtrlTab_DeleteAllItems($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: True
Failure: False

###Remarks###
This does not delete the controls on the tabitems
