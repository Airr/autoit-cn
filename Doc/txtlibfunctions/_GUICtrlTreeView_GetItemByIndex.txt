###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetItemByIndex

###Description###
Retrieve a item by its position in the list of child items

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetItemByIndex($hWnd, $hItem, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
$hItem
	Handle to the item
$iIndex
	Zero based index of item in the list of child items
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Handle of the item
Failure: 0

###Remarks###
None.
