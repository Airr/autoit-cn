###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlListView_GetColumnOrder

###Description###
检索当前列的左到右顺序,返回分隔的序号字符串.

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiListView.au3>
_GUICtrlListView_GetColumnOrder($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	控件句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功:	返回分隔的序号字符串
失败:	返回 $LV_ERR
@@End@@

###Remarks###
None.

###Related###
_GUICtrlListView_SetColumnOrder, _GUICtrlListView_SetColumnOrderArray
