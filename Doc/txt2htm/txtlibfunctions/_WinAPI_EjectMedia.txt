###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_EjectMedia

###Description###
设备弹出媒介.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_EjectMedia ( $sDrive )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$sDrive

@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功:	返回 1.

@@End@@
