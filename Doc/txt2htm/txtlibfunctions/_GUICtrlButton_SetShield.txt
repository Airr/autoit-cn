###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlButton_SetShield

###Description###
在指定按钮或链接上显示盾牌图标

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiButton.au3>
_GUICtrlButton_SetShield($hWnd[, $fRequired = True])

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
 $hWnd  控件句柄
 $fRequired  True为设置否则False
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功: True
失败: False
@@End@@

###Remarks###
最低操作系统: Windows Vista
