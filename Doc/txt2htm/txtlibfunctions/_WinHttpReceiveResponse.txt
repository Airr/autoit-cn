###User Defined Function###
_WinHttpReceiveResponse

###Description###
等待接收由WinHttpSendRequest()初始化的HTTP请求的应答

###Syntax###
#Include <WinHttp.au3>
_WinHttpReceiveResponse($hRequest)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hRequest
	由_WinHttpOpenRequest()返回并由_WinHttpSendRequest()发送的句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功:	返回1设置@error 0
失败:	返回0设置@error 且当其为1时表示DllCall失败
@@End@@

###Remarks###
在_WinHttpQueryDataAvailable()及_WinHttpReadData()前必须先调用_WinHttpReceiveResponse()
