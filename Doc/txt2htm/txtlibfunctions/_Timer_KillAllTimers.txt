###User Defined Function###
_Timer_KillAllTimers

###Description###
销毁所有计时器

###Syntax###
#Include <Timers.au3>
_Timer_KillAllTimers($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
 $hWnd  与计时器相关的窗体句柄. 该值必须与传递到创建计时器的_Timer_SetTimer函数的hWnd值相同
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功: 真
失败: 假
@@End@@

###Remarks###
_Timer_KillAllTimers函数不会移除已发送到消息队列的WM_TIMER消息

###Related###
 _Timer_KillTimer , _Timer_SetTimer
