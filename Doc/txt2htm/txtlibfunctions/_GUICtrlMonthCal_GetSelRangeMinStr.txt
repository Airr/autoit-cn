###User Defined Function###

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiMonthCal.au3>
_GUICtrlMonthCal_GetSelRangeMinStr($hWnd[, $sFormat = "%02d/%02d/%04d"])

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	控件句柄
$sFormat
	[可选参数] 用于格式化日期的格式字符串
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###

@@End@@
