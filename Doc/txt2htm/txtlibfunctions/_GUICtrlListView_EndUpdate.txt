###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlListView_EndUpdate

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiListView.au3>
_GUICtrlListView_EndUpdate($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###

@@End@@
