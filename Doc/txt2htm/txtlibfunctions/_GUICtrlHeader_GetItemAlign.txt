###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlHeader_GetItemAlign

###Description###
Retrieves the item text alignment

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiHeader.au3>
_GUICtrlHeader_GetItemAlign($hWnd, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$iIndex
	Zero based item index
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Item text alignment:
		0 - Left
		1 - Right
		2 - Center
Failure: -1

###Remarks###
None.
