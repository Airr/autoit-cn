###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_SetInformationJobObject

###Description###
设置作业对象的限制.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_SetInformationJobObject ( $hJob, $iJobObjectInfoClass, $tJobObjectInfo )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hJob
	作业对象句柄.句柄必须有
		 $JOB_OBJECT_SET_ATTRIBUTES --- 调用 SetInformationJobObject 函数设置作业对象的属性 访问权限.
$iJobObjectInfoClass
	指定限制类别.此参数指定为 $tJobObjectInfo 结构类型, 有效值为:
		 of $tJobObjectInfo structure, valid values:
		2 - $tagJOBOBJECT_BASIC_LIMIT_INFORMATION --- JOBOBJECT_BASIC_LIMIT_INFORMATION 结构的指针
		4 - $tagJOBOBJECT_BASIC_UI_RESTRICTIONS --- JOBOBJECT_BASIC_UI_RESTRICTIONS 结构的指针
		5 - $tagJOBOBJECT_SECURITY_LIMIT_INFORMATION --- JOBOBJECT_SECURITY_LIMIT_INFORMATION 结构的指针
		6 - $tagJOBOBJECT_END_OF_JOB_TIME_INFORMATION --- JOBOBJECT_END_OF_JOB_TIME_INFORMATION 结构的指针
		7 - $tagJOBOBJECT_ASSOCIATE_COMPLETION_PORT --- JOBOBJECT_ASSOCIATE_COMPLETION_PORT 结构的指针
		9 - $tagJOBOBJECT_EXTENDED_LIMIT_INFORMATION --- JOBOBJECT_EXTENDED_LIMIT_INFORMATION 结构的指针
		11 - $tagJOBOBJECT_GROUP_INFORMATION --- 指向 USHORT 值的指针
$tJobObjectInfo
	$tagJOBOBJECT_* 结构, 指定限制和作业状态信息.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功:	返回 1.
失败:	返回 0,并设置@error标志为非 0 值.
@@End@@
