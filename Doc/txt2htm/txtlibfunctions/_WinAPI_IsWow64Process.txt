###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_IsWow64Process

###Description###
Determines whether the specified process is running under WOW64.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_IsWow64Process ( [$PID] )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$PID
	[可选参数] The PID of the process. Default (0) is the current process.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	1 - The process is running under WOW64.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;0	Otherwise.
Failure	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@
