###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlListView_GetItemCut

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiListView.au3>
_GUICtrlListView_GetItemCut($hWnd, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
 $hWnd  控件句柄
 $iIndex  项目的0基索引
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@

假: 项目未被标记
@@End@@
