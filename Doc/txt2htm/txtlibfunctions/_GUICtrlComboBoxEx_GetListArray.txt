###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_GetListArray

###Description###
Retrieves all items from the list portion of a ComboBox control

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiComboBoxEx.au3>
_GUICtrlComboBoxEx_GetListArray($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: Array in the following format:
		[0] - Number of items
		[1] - Item 1
		[2] - Item 2
		[n] - Item n

###Remarks###
None.
