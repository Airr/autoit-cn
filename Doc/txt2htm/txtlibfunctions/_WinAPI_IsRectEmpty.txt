###Function###
_WinAPI_IsRectEmpty

###Description###
Determines whether the specified rectangle is empty.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_IsRectEmpty ( $tRECT )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$tRECT
	$tagRECT structure that contains the logical coordinates of the rectangle.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	1 - The rectangle is empty.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;0	Otherwise.
Failure	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@

###Remarks###
An empty rectangle is one that has no areathat is, the coordinate of the right side is less than or equal
to the coordinate of the left side, or the coordinate of the bottom side is less than or equal to the
coordinate of the top side.
