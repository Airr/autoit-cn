###Function###
_WinAPI_LoadMedia

###Description###
Loads media into a device.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_LoadMedia ( $sDrive )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$sDrive
	The drive letter of the CD tray to load, in the format D:, E:, etc.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	1.
Failure	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@

###Remarks###
None
