###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetNextChild

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include<GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetNextChild($hWnd, $hItem)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@

 $hItem  子项目句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@

@@End@@
