###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_IsClassName

###Description###
包装检查类控件的名称.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPI.au3>
_WinAPI_IsClassName($hWnd, $sClassName)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	控件句柄
$sClassName
	检查的类名
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
	True:	$sClassName 匹配 $hWnd
	False:	$sClassName 不匹配 $hWnd
@@End@@

###Remarks###
Used for checking correct $hWnd is passed into function
