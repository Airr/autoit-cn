###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlSlider_SetThumbLength

###Description###
设置滑动条长度

###Syntax###
#Include<GuiSlider.au3>
_GUICtrlSlider_SetThumbLength($hWnd, $iLength)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
 $hWnd  控件句柄
 $iLength  滑动条像素长度
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
无.
@@End@@

###Remarks###
当轨道条不具有$TBS_FIXEDLENGTH样式时函数无效
