###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlSlider_SetThumbLength

###Description###
设置滑块(指针)长度.
原文:Sets the length of the slider

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiSlider.au3>
_GUICtrlSlider_SetThumbLength($hWnd, $iLength)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	控件句柄
$iLength
	滑块(指针)长度像素值
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
None.

###Remarks###
 如果控件没有 $TBS_FIXEDLENGTH 样式,本函数被忽略
