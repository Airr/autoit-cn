###Function###
_WinAPI_EqualRgn

###Description###
Checks the two specified regions to determine whether they are identical.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_EqualRgn ( $hRgn1, $hRgn2 )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hRgn1
	Handle to a region.
$hRgn2
	Handle to a region.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	1 - The two regions are equal.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;0	Otherwise.
Failure	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@
