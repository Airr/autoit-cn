###Function###
_WinAPI_ColorHLSToRGB

###Description###
Converts colors from hue-luminance-saturation (HLS) to RGB format.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_ColorHLSToRGB ( $iHue, $iLuminance, $iSaturation )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$iHue
	HLS hue value.
$iLuminance
	HLS luminance value.
$iSaturation
	HLS saturation value.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	Returns the RGB value.
Failure	(-1) and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@

None
