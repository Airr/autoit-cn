###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlComboBox_GetDroppedState

###Description###
确定组合框的列表箱是否打开

###Syntax###
#include <GuiComboBox.au3>
_GUICtrlComboBox_GetDroppedState($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	控件的控件ID/句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
True: 展开 
False: 未展开

###Remarks###
None.
