###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlHeader_SetFilterChangeTimeout

###Description###
Sets the filter change timeout interval

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiHeader.au3>
_GUICtrlHeader_SetFilterChangeTimeout($hWnd, $iTimeOut)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$iTimeOut
	Timeout value, in milliseconds
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: The index of the filter control being modified
Failure: 0
