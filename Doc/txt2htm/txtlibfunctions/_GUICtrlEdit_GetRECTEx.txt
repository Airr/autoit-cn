###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlEdit_GetRECTEx

###Description###
Retrieves the formatting rectangle of an edit control

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiEdit.au3>
_GUICtrlEdit_GetRECTEx($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: $tagRECT structure that recieves formatting rectangle of an edit control
