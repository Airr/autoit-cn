###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_BitmapCreateFromHBITMAP

###Description###
从位图句柄创建Bitmap对象

###Syntax###
#Include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_BitmapCreateFromHBITMAP($hBmp[, $hPal = 0])

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hBmp 
   HBITMAP句柄
$hPal 
   HPALETTE句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功: Bitmap对象句柄
失败: 0
@@End@@

###Remarks###
Bitmap对象使用完毕后, 调用_WinAPI_DeleteObject释放资源

###Related###
 _WinAPI_DeleteObject
