###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlTab_ActivateTab
###Description###
Activates a tab by its index
###Syntax###
#Include <GuiTab.au3>
_GUICtrlTab_ActivateTab($hWnd, $iIndex)
###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$iIndex
	Specifies the zero based index of the item
@@End@@
###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success:	The index of the previously selected tab
Failure:	-1
@@End@@

None.
