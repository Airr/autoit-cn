###User Defined Function###
_ScreenCapture_SetTIFColorDepth

###Description###
设置用于TIFF屏幕捕捉的色深

###Syntax###
#Include<ScreenCapture.au3>
_ScreenCapture_SetTIFColorDepth($iDepth)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
 $iDepth  图像色深:
 0 - 默认编码的色深
24 - 24 bit
32 - 32
  bit
@@End@@

###Remarks###
如果未设置, TIFF屏幕捕捉默认到 24 bits
