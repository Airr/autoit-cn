###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_SwapQWord

###Description###
Converts a ULONGLONG from little-endian to big-endian, and vice versa.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_SwapQWord ( $iValue )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$iValue
	The ULONGLONG value to convert.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	The converted value.
Failure	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@
