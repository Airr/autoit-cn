###User Defined Function###
_IsPressed

###Description###
检查按键是否被按下

###Syntax###
#Include <Misc.au3>
_IsPressed($sHexKey[, $vDLL ='user32.dll'])

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
 $sHexKey  要检查的键
 $vDLL  dll 句柄或默认 user32.dll
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
真: 1
假: 0
@@End@@

###Remarks###
如果重复调用, 应该打开'user32.dll'并传递句柄.
脚本结束时请确定将之关闭
