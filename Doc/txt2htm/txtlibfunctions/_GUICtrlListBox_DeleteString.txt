###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlListBox_DeleteString

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include<GuiListBox.au3>
_GUICtrlListBox_DeleteString($hWnd, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
 $hWnd  控件句柄
 $iIndex  被删除字符串的0基索引
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@

@@End@@
