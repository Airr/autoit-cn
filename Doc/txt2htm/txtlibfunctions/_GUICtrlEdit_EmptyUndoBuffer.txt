###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlEdit_EmptyUndoBuffer

###Description###
重置编辑控件撤消标志

###Syntax###
#include <GuiEdit.au3>
_GUICtrlEdit_EmptyUndoBuffer($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	控件的控件ID/句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
None.

###Remarks###
 每当编辑控件接收一个_GUICtrlEdit_SetText 操作,撤消标志自动复位.

###Related###
_GUICtrlEdit_CanUndo, _GUICtrlEdit_Undo
