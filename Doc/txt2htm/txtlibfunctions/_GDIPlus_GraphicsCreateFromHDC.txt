###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_GraphicsCreateFromHDC

###Description###
由设备场景(DC)创建Graphics对象

###Syntax###
#Include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_GraphicsCreateFromHDC($hDC)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hDC 
   设备场景句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功: Graphics对象句柄
失败: 0
@@End@@

###Remarks###
处理完Graphics对象后, 调用_GDIPlus_GraphicsDispose释放资源

###Related###
 _GDIPlus_GraphicsDispose
