###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlHeader_GetItemRectEx

###Description###
Retrieves the bounding rectangle for a given item

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiHeader.au3>
_GUICtrlHeader_GetItemRectEx($hWnd, $iIndex)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to control
$iIndex
	Zero based item index
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
Success: $tagRECT structure that receives the bounding rectangle

###Remarks###
None.
