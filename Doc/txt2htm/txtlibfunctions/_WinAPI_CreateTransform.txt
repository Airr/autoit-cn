###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_CreateTransform

###Description###
Ceates $tagXFORM structure specifies a world-space to page-space transformation.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_CreateTransform ( [$nM11 [, $nM12 [, $nM21 [, $nM22 [, $nDX [, $nDY]]]]]] )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$nM11
	[可选参数] The following.
	
	Rotation   - Cosine of rotation angle.
	Scaling    - Horizontal scaling component.
	Shear      - Not used.
	Reflection - Horizontal component.
	
$nM12
	[可选参数] The following.
	
	Rotation   - Sine of the rotation angle.
	Scaling    - Not used.
	Shear      - Horizontal proportionality constant.
	Reflection - Not used.
	
$nM21
	[可选参数] The following.
	
	Rotation   - Negative sine of the rotation angle.
	Scaling    - Not used.
	Shear      - Vertical proportionality constant.
	Reflection - Not used.
	
$nM22
	[可选参数] The following.
	
	Rotation   - Cosine of rotation angle.
	Scaling    - Vertical scaling component.
	Shear      - Not used.
	Reflection - Vertical reflection component.
	
$nDx
	[可选参数] The horizontal translation component, in logical units.
$nDy
	[可选参数] The vertical translation component, in logical units.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	$tagXFORM structure that contains the transformation data.
失败:	返回 0 并设置 @error 标志为非 0 值.
@@End@@
