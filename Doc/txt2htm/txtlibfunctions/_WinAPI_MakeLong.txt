###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_MakeLong

###Description###
从两个整数值(Int)返回一个长整值(LongInt).

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPI.au3>
_WinAPI_MakeLong($iLo, $iHi)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$iLo
	Low word
$iHi
	Hi word
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
返回长整值

###Remarks###
None.

###Related###
_WinAPI_HiWord, _WinAPI_LoWord
