###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_ClientToScreen

###Description###
将指定点的用户坐标转换成屏幕坐标

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPI.au3>
_WinAPI_ClientToScreen($hWnd,
ByRef $tPoint)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
   用于转换的窗口句柄
$tPoint
$tagPOINT结构的指针
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功: $tagPOINT结构
@@End@@

###Remarks###
函数用屏幕坐标取代$tagPOINT结构. 屏幕坐标与屏幕左上角相关联.

###Related###
_WinAPI_ScreenToClient , $tagPOINT
