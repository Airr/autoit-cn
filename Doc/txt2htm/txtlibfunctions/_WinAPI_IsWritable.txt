###Function###
_WinAPI_IsWritable

###Description###
Determines whether a disk is writable.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_IsWritable ( $sDrive )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$sDrive
	The drive letter of the disk to check, in the format D:, E:, etc.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	1 - The disk is writable.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;0	Otherwise.
Failure	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@
