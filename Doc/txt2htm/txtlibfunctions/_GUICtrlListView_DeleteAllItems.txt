###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlListView_DeleteAllItems

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiListView.au3>
_GUICtrlListView_DeleteAllItems($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	控件的控件ID/句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功:	返回 True
失败:	返回 False
@@End@@
