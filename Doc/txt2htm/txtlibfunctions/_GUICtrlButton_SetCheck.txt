###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlButton_SetCheck

###Description###
设置单选按钮或复选框的选取状态

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiButton.au3>
_GUICtrlButton_SetCheck($hWnd[, $iState = $BST_CHECKED]])

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
 $hWnd  控件句柄
 $iState  选取状态.这个参数可能是下列值之一:
$BST_CHECKED - 设置按钮状态为选中.
$BST_INDETERMINATE - 设置按钮状态为灰色表明不确定状态.只有当按钮有$BS_3STATE或$BS_AUTO3STATE样式才能使用这个参数.
$BST_UNCHECKED - 设置按钮为非选取状态.
@@End@@

###Remarks###
_GUICtrlButton_SetCheck对按钮无效.

###Related###
_GUICtrlButton_GetCheck , _GUICtrlButton_GetState , _GUICtrlButton_SetState
