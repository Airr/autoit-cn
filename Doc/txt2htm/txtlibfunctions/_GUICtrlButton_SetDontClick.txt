###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlButton_SetDontClick

###Description###
设置按钮的 $BST_DONTCLICK 状态标志

###Syntax###
#include <GuiButton.au3>
_GUICtrlButton_SetDontClick($hWnd [, $fState = True])

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	控件的控件ID/句柄
$fState
	[可选参数] 状态.如为 True, 则设置 $BST_DONTCLICK, 否则为 False
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
None.

###Remarks###
最低操作系统: Windows Vista.
