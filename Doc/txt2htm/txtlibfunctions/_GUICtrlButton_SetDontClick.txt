###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlButton_SetDontClick

###Description###
设置按钮$BST_DONTCLICK标记的状态

###Syntax###
#Include<GuiButton.au3>
_GUICtrlButton_SetDontClick($hWnd[, $fState = True])

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
 $hWnd  控件句柄
 $fState  可选状态. True - 设置$BST_DONTCLICK标记 , 不设置时为False
@@End@@

###Remarks###
最低操作系统: Windows Vista
