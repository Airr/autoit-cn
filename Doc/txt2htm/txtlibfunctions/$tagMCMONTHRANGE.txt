###结构常量###
$tagMCMONTHRANGE

###Description###
获取代表在月历控件中日期显示的高低限信息

###Syntax###
Global Const $tagMCMONTHRANGE =  "short MinYear;short MinMonth;short MinDOW;short MinDay;short MinHour;short MinMinute;short MinSecond;short MinMSeconds;short MaxYear;short MaxMonth;short MaxDOW;short MaxDay;short MaxHour;short MaxMaxute;short MaxSecond;short MaxMSeconds;short Span"
