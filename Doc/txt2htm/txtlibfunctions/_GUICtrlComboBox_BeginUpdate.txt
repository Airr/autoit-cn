###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlComboBox_BeginUpdate

###Description###
Prevents updating of the control until the EndUpdate function is called

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiComboBox.au3>
_GUICtrlComboBox_BeginUpdate($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd
	Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
成功: True
失败: False

###Remarks###
None.
