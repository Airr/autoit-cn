_GUICtrlTreeView_GetChecked

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include<GuiTreeView.au3>
_GUICtrlTreeView_GetChecked($hWnd, $hItem)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@

 $hItem  项目句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@

假: 项目复选框未被选中
