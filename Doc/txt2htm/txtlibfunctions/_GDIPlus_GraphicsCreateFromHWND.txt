###User Defined Function###
_GDIPlus_GraphicsCreateFromHWND

###Description###
由窗体句柄创建Graphics对象

###Syntax###
#Include <GDIPlus.au3>
_GDIPlus_GraphicsCreateFromHWND($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd 
   窗体句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功: Graphics对象句柄
失败: 0
@@End@@

###Remarks###
处理完Graphics对象后, 调用_GDIPlus_GraphicsDispose释放资源

###Related###
 _GDIPlus_GraphicsDispose
