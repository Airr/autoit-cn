###用户自定义函数###
_GUICtrlRichEdit_CanRedo

###描述###
确定撤销动作能否重做

###语法###
#Include <GuiRichEdit.au3>
_GUICtrlRichEdit_CanRedo($hWnd)

###参数###
@@ParamTable@@
 $hWnd  Handle to the control
@@End@@

###返回值###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功: 真或假
失败: 假并设置@error
    101 - $hWnd不是句柄
@@End@@

###备注###

###相关###
 _GUICtrlRichEdit_Redo , _GUICtrlRichEdit_SetUndoLimit
