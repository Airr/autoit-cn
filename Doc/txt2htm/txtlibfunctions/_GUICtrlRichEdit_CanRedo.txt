###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlRichEdit_CanRedo

###Description###
确定撤销动作能否重做

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiRichEdit.au3>
_GUICtrlRichEdit_CanRedo($hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
 $hWnd  Handle to the control
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功: 真或假
失败: 假并设置@error
    101 - $hWnd不是句柄
@@End@@
