###Function###
_WinAPI_GetCaretPos

###Description###
Retrieves the caret's position.

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_GetCaretPos ( )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
None.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
Success	The array that contains the client coordinates of the caret.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;[0]	x-coordinate of the caret.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;[1]	y-coordinate of the caret.
Failure	0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero.
@@End@@
