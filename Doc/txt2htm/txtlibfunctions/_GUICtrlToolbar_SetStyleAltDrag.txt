###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlToolbar_SetStyleAltDrag

###Description###
设置控件是否允许拖曳按钮

###Syntax###
#Include<GuiToolBar.au3>
_GUICtrlToolbar_SetStyleAltDrag($hWnd[, $fState =True])

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
 $hWnd  控件句柄
 $fState  True为设置, False为不设置
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功: True
失败: False
@@End@@
