###User Defined Function###
_WinAPI_DeleteVolumeMountPoint

###Description###
删除驱动器号或挂载文件夹

###Syntax###
#Include <WinAPIEx.au3>
_WinAPI_DeleteVolumeMountPoint( $sPath )

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$sPath
   要被删除的驱动器号或挂载文件夹(如X:\或Y:\MountX\)
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功: 返回1
失败: 返回0并设置@error非0
@@End@@

###Remarks###
删除挂载点不会删除底层文件夹
