###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlAVI_Destroy

###Description###

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiAVI.au3>
_GUICtrlAVI_Destroy(ByRef$hWnd)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$hWnd 
   控件句柄
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
成功: 真 , 句柄被置为 0
失败: 假
@@End@@
