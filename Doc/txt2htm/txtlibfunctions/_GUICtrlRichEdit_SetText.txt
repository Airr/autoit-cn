###User Defined Function###
_GUICtrlRichEdit_SetText

###Description###
设置控件文本

###Syntax###
#Include <GuiRichEdit.au3>
_GUICtrlRichEdit_SetText($hWnd, $sText)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
 $hWnd  控件句柄
 $sText  置于控件内的纯文本或富文本
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
@@ReturnTable@@
 成功:  真
 失败:  假并设置@error
 @error:  101 - $hWnd不是句柄
@@End@@

###Remarks###
设置所有文本. 文本可为纯文本或富文本.
保留撤销块

###Related###
 _GUICtrlRichEdit_GetText , _GUICtrlRichEdit_AppendText , _GUICtrlRichEdit_InsertText , _GUICtrlRichEdit_ReplaceText , _GUICtrlRichEdit_EmptyUndoBuffer

 #AutoIt3Wrapper_Au3Check_Parameters= -d -w 1 -w 2 -w 3 -w 4 -w 5 -w 6

 Func Main ()
     Local  $hGui ,  $iMsg ,  $hRichEdit
     $hGui  =  GUICreate ( "Example ("  &amp;  StringTrimRight ( @ScriptName , 4 )  &amp; ")" ,  320 ,  350 ,  - 1 ,  - 1 )

     While  True
         $iMsg  =  GUIGetMsg ()

             Case  $iMsg  =  $GUI_EVENT_CLOSE
                 GUIDelete ()
                 Exit
         EndSelect
     WEnd
